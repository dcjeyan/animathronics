Welcome to Independent and Dependent Variables

Variable
A variable is a symbol that may take different values. It is usually represented by letters like a, b, m, n, p, x, and y.

Independent Variable
It is a variable that stands alone and whose value does not depend on the other variable. It may also refer as the input of the function.

Dependent Variable
It is something that depends on other factors. It is affected by the changes on the independent variable. It may also refer as the output of the function.

FOR EXAMPLE: A.) Karina counted the number of books in front of her to calculate how long would it take her to read all of it.

Independent Variable:The number of books that karina would read
Dependent Variable:how long would it take her to read all of it

FOR EXAMPLE: B.) Giselle checked all of her unaccomplished task to estimate how much time would it take her to finish her outputs.

Independent Variable:All of her unaccomplished task that needs to be finished
Dependent Variable:How much time would it take her to finish her outputs

FOR EXAMPLE: C.)Before going back home, Yizhuo decided to measure how much time would it took her to go to her destination while driving her bike at a constant speed

Independent Variable:Driving her bike at a constant speed
Dependent Variable:How much time would it took her to go to her destinations
